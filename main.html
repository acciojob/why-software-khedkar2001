Why I want to become a developer?

Since childhood, I have been fascinated by technology and how software can solve real-world problems. The ability to create something impactful from just lines of code excites me. My curiosity led me to explore programming, and over time, I developed a deep passion for software development. I enjoy the logical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity involved in coding. Becoming a developer allows me to turn ideas into reality, build innovative applications, and contribute to technological advancements that improve people's lives.
